Bio

Blending Garage, UK Funky, and Broken Beat, Murder He Wrote’s playful productions and edits have become a mainstay of clubs worldwide. Breaking out via Roska Kicks & Snares, his tracks have gained support from tastemakers including Ben UFO, Jyoty, Fred Again, Jamz Supernova, and Mary Anne Hobbs.

Working across 3 decks, his low-end focused DJ sets have featured on BBC Radio 1, Boiler Room, NTS, Rinse FM, and clubs/festivals around the world including Fabric, Ministry Of Sound, and Outlook.

Recent highlights include releases on DJ Zinc’s Bingo Beats and Jamz Supernova’s Future Bounce, collaborations with Roska, official remixes for Afro B and T-Pain, and multiple Spotify New Music Friday features.
